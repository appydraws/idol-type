The Discovery

Your journey through the neon tunnels ends abruptly in a sudden clearing. The air hums with a new energy. At your feet lies a shimmering object. You pick it up, and it unfolds into a golden ticket, pulsing with a soft, inviting light. As you hold it, the dense, crystalline walls around you give way to a vast, luminous forest. Giant, glowing flora stretches towards a sky filled with a kaleidoscope of floating orbs. A faint, hypnotic melody drifts through the air, drawing you in.
