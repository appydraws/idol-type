A Luminous Discovery

You can't resist your curiosity. You quickly unzip your bag and peer inside. Nestled amongst your belongings is a luminous, holographic **Golden Ticket**. You have no idea how it got there or what it does. A powerful mix of excitement and unease swirls inside you, but your curiosity wins. You wave the ticket around, and to your surprise, its light glows stronger, pulling you in a particular direction. You decide to follow this strange pull and see where it leads.
